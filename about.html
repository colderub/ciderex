Our Story

Discover the passion, tradition, and craftsmanship behind every bottle of ETUB Cider

The Beginning

ETUB Cider was born from a simple belief: that exceptional cider comes from exceptional apples and unwavering dedication to traditional craftsmanship. What started as a passionate experiment in small-batch brewing has evolved into a celebration of apple heritage and artisanal excellence.

Our founders discovered that the ancient art of cider making, when combined with modern quality standards and innovative flavor profiles, could create something truly extraordinary. Each bottle represents our commitment to honoring tradition while embracing innovation.

"Every apple tells a story. Every bottle captures a moment in time."

- ETUB Founders

Our Craft

Traditional cider making is both an art and a science. We carefully select premium apples from trusted orchards, ensuring each variety contributes its unique character to our final blend. Our fermentation process respects time-honored techniques while incorporating modern quality control.

From the crushing of the first apple to the final bottling, every step is monitored with meticulous attention to detail. We believe that patience and precision are the secret ingredients that transform simple apple juice into extraordinary cider.

Our small-batch approach allows us to maintain the highest quality standards while experimenting with seasonal flavors and innovative combinations that surprise and delight cider enthusiasts.
